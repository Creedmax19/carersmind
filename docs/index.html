Who We Support

Providing resources and community for all types of carers

Professional Care Workers

Home carers, support workers, healthcare assistants, and all professionals in the care sector.

Family & Informal Carers

Those supporting loved ones with illness, disability, or age-related needs.

Immigrant Carers

Supporting those working in health and social care away from their home country.

Our Mission

Creating a supportive environment for carers across the UK

"To promote and protect the mental health of carers in the UK through emotional support, education, peer connection, and community-led advocacy."

What We Do

Comprehensive support programs for carers

Peer Support Groups

Safe, inclusive sessions for sharing experiences and emotional support.

Mental Health Sharing

Exchange self-care tips, stress-relief strategies, and coping mechanisms.

Expert Workshops

Sessions with therapists and mental health professionals.

MHFA Training

Access to Mental Health First Aid certification programs.

Awareness Campaigns

Highlighting carers' challenges and contributions to society.

Crisis Support

Guidance to NHS services, helplines, and resources.

Future Initiatives

Expanding our support as we grow

  • One-to-one mental health check-ins for carers
  • Wellbeing packs with journals and relaxation tools
  • Retreat or rest days for overwhelmed carers
  • Mentorship and leadership pathways
